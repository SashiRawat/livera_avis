Welcome to LIBERA AVIS

"Libera Avis" is a Latin phrase that translates to "Free Bird." It represents the concept of freedom and liberation, combining the words "libera," meaning "free," and "avis," meaning "bird." This name has been chosen for a startup that aims to provide a platform for upcoming artists to showcase their talent.

The purpose of Libera Avis is to offer a stage or platform for emerging artists, allowing them to express themselves freely and fully. The startup recognizes the importance of providing opportunities and exposure to talented individuals who are often overlooked in the mainstream industry. By giving these artists a platform, Libera Avis aims to help them unleash their creative potential and gain recognition for their work.

To support this mission, Libera Avis plans to collaborate with venue partners. These partnerships will involve promoting the cafes and the artists simultaneously, creating a mutually beneficial arrangement. The cafes will serve as venues for showcasing the artists' talents, providing them with an audience and exposure. In return, the cafes will benefit from the increased foot traffic and the added cultural appeal that comes with supporting the arts.

The tagline of Libera Avis is "A Way to Express." This tagline reflects the startup's belief in the power of expression and the importance of providing a platform where artists can freely and completely express themselves. Libera Avis aims to break down barriers and create an environment where artists feel empowered to share their unique perspectives and artistic visions.

Overall, Libera Avis is a startup that seeks to support upcoming artists by offering them a stage to showcase their talent. Through collaborations with cafes and a commitment to freedom of expression, Libera Avis aims to provide artists with the opportunities they need to thrive and make their voices heard in the creative industry.

About Us

Our tagline "A Way to Express" reflects our belief in breaking barriers and empowering artists to share their unique perspectives.
